Art

I love to draw in my sketchbook which comes with me almost everywhere. For as long as I can remember, I have always had a passion for art. I took a few courses at Penn State University to gain some formal education as well as techniques unknown to me.

Currently, I am a part-time screen printer at Premier Screen Printing while I attend school. I have been screen printing professionally for the last six years and love it!
